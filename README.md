### 1. Upload the files
Make sure all the files I just gave you (including the `.github` folder) are uploaded to your GitHub repository.

### 2. Enable GitHub Actions Hosting
This is the most important part:
1.  Go to your repository on GitHub.com.
2.  Click the **Settings** tab at the top.
3.  On the left menu, click **Pages**.
4.  Under **Build and deployment** > **Source**, change the dropdown from "Deploy from a branch" to **"GitHub Actions"**.

### 3. Wait and Share!
1.  Click the **Actions** tab at the top of your repository. You should see a workflow running named "Deploy to GitHub Pages".
2.  Wait for it to finish (it will turn green).
3.  Go back to **Settings > Pages**, and you will see your live link!
